Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to add a new listing?
We review all requests manually and aim to process submissions within 2–4 weeks. Complex additions may take longer.

Can I request removal of a listing?
Yes. If you are the maker or their representative and wish to be removed from the database, please contact us and we will process the removal promptly.

Do you accept advertising or sponsored listings?
No. All listings are selected editorially to maintain the independence of this resource. We do not accept payment for placement.

I found incorrect information. What happens next?
We take accuracy seriously. Verified corrections are typically applied within one week of receipt.
